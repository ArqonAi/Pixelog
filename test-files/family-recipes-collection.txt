FAMILY RECIPE COLLECTION

Grandma's Chocolate Chip Cookies:
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups chocolate chips
Bake 375°F for 10-12 minutes

Mom's Famous Lasagna:
- 1 lb ground beef
- Lasagna noodles
- Ricotta cheese
- Mozzarella cheese
- Tomato sauce
- Italian seasoning
Layer and bake 350°F for 45 minutes

Dad's BBQ Sauce:
- 1 cup ketchup
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- Garlic powder and onion powder

Passed down through 3 generations!
